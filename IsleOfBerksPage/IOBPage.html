    Our History

    Nestled amidst the turbulent seas, Isle of Berk stands as a testament to the enduring spirit of its Viking inhabitants. For generations, they have weathered storms, forged alliances, and carved out a life steeped in tradition and valor. The rugged cliffs and verdant valleys of Berk bear witness to their resilience, each fjord echoing with the songs of their ancestors. Here, on this remote outpost, the Viking way of life thrives, untouched by the passage of time.

    But the tranquil rhythm of Berk's existence is shattered with the arrival of dragons. What ensues is a clash of titans, as Vikings and dragons engage in a fierce battle for dominance, leaving the island scarred and its people shaken. Yet, from the ashes of conflict, a fragile peace begins to take root. The once-feared creatures become an integral part of Berk's landscape, soaring through the skies alongside their former adversaries. As bonds form and animosities fade, Isle of Berk transforms into a sanctuary where human and dragon coexist, bound by a shared destiny.

    Our Master

    Amidst this newfound harmony, a figure emerges from the shadows, wielding a power that could shape the fate of Berk forever. Zenithrax, the enigmatic Dragon Master, steps forward, his bond with the mighty fire dragon, Igris, setting him apart as a pioneer in the art of dragon communication. With unwavering determination, he founds the Dragon Academy, a beacon of hope in a world still grappling with the scars of war. Through his teachings, the people of Berk learn to harness the innate abilities of their newfound allies, forging deeper connections and unlocking the true potential of their partnership.

    As the years pass, Berk undergoes a remarkable transformation, evolving into a bustling hub of diversity and innovation. Drawn by tales of the Zen Dragon Academy's success, newcomers from distant shores flock to the island, each bringing their unique skills and perspectives. Together, they breathe new life into Berk, shaping it into a melting pot of cultures and ideas. The Dragon Academy, once a symbol of survival, now stands as a testament to unity and progress, its halls teeming with students eager to learn the ways of the dragons. In this ever-changing landscape, Isle of Berk continues to thrive, guided by the enduring bond between man and dragon.
